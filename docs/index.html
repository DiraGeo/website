Dira GeoSystems

Dira GeoSystems develops software for the efficient production of highly accurate geodata and beautiful maps. We specialize both in the ergonomics of the user experience and high-performance algorithms to boost the productivity of data production and processing. Our main products are built on the ProSuite software platform that has been developed for more than a decade for interactive data capture and editing, quality assurance, and cartographic refinement. The ProSuite platform is currently used by a variety of organisations ranging from the national mapping agencies of Switzerland, Germany, and Austria to medium and small organisations, such as the GIS departments at the state and local level or private companies.

Dira GeoSystems has been founded in 2021 with the mission to build and support the ProSuite tools for ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Enterprise. Additionally, we support large and small organisations during and after the migration to ArcGIS Pro leveraging our experience with large data models and our expertise in cartography.

Products

Dira GeoSystems engineers the following products to the highest customer satisfaction:

Dira ProSuite

Dira GeoSystem's ProSuite is a suite of high-end productivity tools for data production, quality assurance, and cartographic refinement, implemented as an extension of Esri's flagship application ArcGIS Pro.

Product Details

LocationFinder

Dira GeoSystem's LocationFinder is a fast and friendly interactive search engine over your address and place data with plentiful configuration possibilities for fine-tuning the end user's search experience.

Services

Dira GeoSystems provides training and consulting services for the Dira ProSuite product family and LocationFinder. The ProSuite codebase is easily integrated into customer-specific solutions, allowing for cost-efficient and rapid development of your software projects.

We are happy to share our vast experience in data production and cartography. Our special expertise covers the following topics:

  • Geodatabase modelling and design
  • Data and map production workflows, geodatabase versioning and (Oracle) database tuning
  • Designing workflows for the processing of very large datasets
  • Software development for ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Enterprise
