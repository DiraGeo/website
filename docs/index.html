DIRA GEOSYSTEMS

Dira GeoSystems is dedicated to developing software for tremendously useful geodata and the most beautiful maps, by putting all our craft and care into our products and projects and sharing our vast experience to the benefit of our customers.

Dira is swahili for compass, an old arabic measure of length (about one meter), and associates with Indira, a byname of the Hindu godess of beauty.

Data and Mapping

Dira GeoSystems develops ProSuite, the productivity extension for ArcGIS for data production, quality assurance, and cartographic refinement.

The new ProSuite is open source. Follow its active development right at the source on GitHub. Your technical question might already be answered in the ProSuite Tech Blog.
