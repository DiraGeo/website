DIRA GEOSYSTEMS

Dira GeoSystems is dedicated to developing software for tremendously useful geodata and the most beautiful maps, by putting all our craft and care into our products and projects and sharing our vast experience to the benefit of our customers.

Dira GeoSystems develops ProSuite, the productivity extension for ArcGIS for data production, quality assurance, and cartographic refinement. The new ProSuite is open source. Follow its active development right at the source on GitHub. Your technical question might already be answered in the ProSuite Tech Blog.

Data

Dira produces software for your tremendously useful geodata.

Our portfolio includes the ProSuite QA Solution for advanced high performance quality checking of your geodata, along with a broad range of highly effective and usable tools for interactive editing. ProSuite runs as an Add-In for ArcGIS Pro but is also accessible through a Python API.

LocationFinder is probably the fastest address and place name search for your data. It features fine grained configuration and normatization options, provides an easy REST interface for your own clients, and seamlessly integrates into the ArcGIS platform.

Mapping

Clean data is a necessary basis for effective cartography, but in itself not sufficient. You need specialised data models for good performance and manageability, and for the utmost quality and beauty you will want some (manual and automatic) refinement. Our ProSuite Carto Solution offers a large range of automatic processes and interactive tools for cartographic refinement. Combine it with Dira's long standing expertise in cartography: we are glad to help.
